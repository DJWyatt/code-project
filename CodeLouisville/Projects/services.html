Services

Classes
Begin your beading experience in this fun-filled, easy and informative class. This class will provide you with all the skills needed to get started. You will make a pair of earrings and a bracelet during class period, as well as get hands-on techniques and experience using tools and findings. The cost includes all materials, use of tools and instruction handout. This One Hour Class is offered Monday - Friday at a cost of $35 per person. It is considered to be a one-on-one private class, but you may want to enjoy it with a friend! Call Tammy to set up a time that is convenient for you! 502-893-6060

Jewelry Repairs
We provide a large variety of jewelry repair, including beaded necklaces, bracelets and earrings. We also do an excellent job in knotting pearls.
