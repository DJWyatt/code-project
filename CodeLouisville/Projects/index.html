Welcome to AfterGlow Beads & Supplies

We are the largest bead store in the Kentuckiana area and have all the products and supplies that you will need to design and make your own jewelry. Our beads come from all over the world and our large inventory includes semi-precious stones, Swarovski Crystals, Pearls, Glass, Wood, Sterling Silver, 14K Goldfilled, Lampwork, cane glass, and much more. We also have a tremendous selection of clasps, earring components, findings, tools, books and kits. Classes are also available to help you learn and grow in your design and technique. In addition to the beading supplies, we also carry mineral specimens which include such things as spheres, amethyst cathedrals, slabs, and both rough and faceted gemstones. We are located next door to our "sister" store: After Glow Wedding Boutique. It's website is AfterGlowWeddingBoutique.com
